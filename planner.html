How to plan your trip with PlanAway.

Use the country selector to choose from one of the 20 most visited countries in the world. Type in any city within your chosen country to view it on the map.

Select 1 of 4 filter searches to show related results on the map.

Use the country info button to bring up additional information relating to your selcted country. Click one of the 3 attractions to visit the sponsored site page.

All sponser site for futher research are easily accessable using the sponser button.
